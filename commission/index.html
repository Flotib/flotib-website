How to commission?

From the very beginning to the end

Conditions

• No explicit NSFW

• Payment through Paypal only (in euro)

• I start the commission only after the payment

• I can refund until I start to work on your artwork

• I can take several days to achieve your render for multiple reasons, but you'll be warned if it happens

• Your artwork will be public but you can be cited as anonymous if you wish

• Discord or Twitter to chat with me

What I need

• What pack did you choose (see below)

• What type of format do you want ? (Landscape or Portrait)

• Armory link + screens of your character (+artworks if you already commissioned an artist), Wowhead dressing room is very appreciated

• Several information about your character, give me as many details as possible (hair color, scars, tattoos...)

• Describe me the pose that you want for your character, feel free to send me few references of the poses you want

• If you paid for a scenery, give me the place where you want your character to be. It can be a specific in-game place, a custom one, an interior...

• Do the same thing for every character that will be in your scene (except npc)
